MADURAI

Madurai, a city steeped in history, and renowned for its grand temples on the banks of the River Vaigai, is a revered pilgrimage site in India.

OOTY

Ooty, nestled in the scenic Nilgiri Mountains, is a serene hill station with lush tea gardens, providing a tranquil getaway in South India.

JAIPUR

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, invites you to explore its royal palaces, vibrant bazaars, and historical forts, immersing you in the grandeur of Indian royalty.

AGRA

Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, showcases a rich Mughal heritage and is a historical gem that draws visitors from around the world.

PARIS

Paris, the "City of Love," with its romantic ambiance, world-class art, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, captures the hearts of millions.

TOKYO

Tokyo, a dynamic metropolis in Japan, seamlessly blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology, creating a unique cultural experience.
